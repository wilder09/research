            Transforming Lives 
            Through Research
            

            
            At Research Made Accessible, we believe that
            education is key to transforming lives. Our 
            mentorship program provides underserved 
            students with the opportunity to learn and 
            grow through research mentorship. By 
            supporting our efforts, you can help us make a
            difference in the lives of these students.
In collaboration with

Stanford University

Rice University

John Hopkins

Princeton University

Texas Tech

Baylor University

Interest

         
          
Are you a high school student 
looking to dive into the world
of academic research? This is
your gateway to an incredible
opportunity to work alongside 
experts in your desired field. Fill 
out the interest form with your
contact and academic interests
to get started. By joining this 
program, you'll gain valuable 
experience and insights that can
shape your future academic and
career paths.

Matchmaking

         
          
After filling out the interest
form, you will be placed on a
waitlist waiting to get connected
with a professor in the field of
your preferred choice. Once
selected, we will reach out to you
via email, please ensure your contact
information is up to date and keep
an eye on your inbox for further 
instructions. We appreciate your 
patience and interest 
in our program.

Research

         
          
Depending on your experience,
you will either be recommended
to learn from introductory
precursors, or delve straight
into getting guidance from the
professor you match with being
able to contact your "mentor"
for help over email, zoom 
meetings, etc. This tailored
approach ensures that you
receive the appropriate level
of support and resources to
succeed in your research
endeavors.
